Coffee Time

Coffee-based micro economies for hackers.

iOS Android

Let me buy you a coffee.

An app for managing coffee debt within your office. Buy your buddy a coffee, and keep track of just how many coffee's that freeloader owes you. Debt is simplified across your group, creating a web of coffee credit. Pick up the tab at lunch and know you're investing in the most stable commodity future available today - coffee.

For everyone in your circle.

We know you have a GitHub account, so you already have a Coffee Time account. Just authorize one of your groups as a Coffee Group, and your scheme to becoming the Office Coffee Baron is off the ground.

Full Service API.

Our Coffee Time API lives on GitHub. Spin up your own additions to the service that are right for your office. Yearly debt jubilees? IRC hooks that shame your co-workers when they get to deep in the hole? Wire it up.
